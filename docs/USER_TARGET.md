# Target User Profile

## Persona: Long-Term Family Investor

### Demographics
- **Age**: 35-50 years old
- **Family Status**: Married with 1-3 children
- **Employment**: Stable middle-class income
- **Investment Horizon**: 15-25+ years (retirement, children's education)

### Financial Goals
1. **Primary**: Protect and grow family wealth for retirement
2. **Secondary**: Build education fund for children
3. **Tertiary**: Save for major life events (home, healthcare buffer)

### Risk Profile
- **Risk Tolerance**: Moderate - wants growth but cannot afford major losses
- **Psychological Profile**: Loss-averse, prefers steady compounding over get-rich-quick schemes
- **Time Horizon**: Long-term (can weather short-term volatility)

### Investment Philosophy
- "Time in the market > timing the market"
- Prefers passive ETF investing over stock picking
- Values diversification and risk management
- Wants to understand but not obsess over daily fluctuations
- Seeks peace of mind through data-driven decisions

### Pain Points
- **Fear**: Losing hard-earned savings (2008 financial crisis trauma)
- **Uncertainty**: "Am I on track for retirement?"
- **Complexity**: Too many options, paralysis by analysis
- **Information Overload**: Conflicting financial advice

### What They Value
1. **Safety**: Capital preservation is paramount
2. **Transparency**: Clear explanation of risks and expected outcomes
3. **Control**: Ability to adjust strategy as life circumstances change
4. **Education**: Understanding why certain allocations are recommended
5. **Scenario Planning**: "What if X happens?" (job loss, market crash, early retirement)

---

## Design Implications

### UI/UX Principles
- **Calm, Reassuring Design**: No alarmist colors or urgent notifications
- **Long-Term Focus**: Emphasize 10+ year horizons, not daily fluctuations
- **Educational Context**: Explain metrics in simple terms
- **Scenario Exploration**: Enable "what-if" without fear of breaking things
- **Historical Reassurance**: Show how strategies performed through past crises

### Key Features for This Persona
1. **Scenario Playground**: Safe space to experiment with allocations
2. **Historical Replay**: "How would this have done in 2008, 2020?"
3. **Goal-Based Planning**: Retirement, education, house fund
4. **Stress Testing**: Simulate market crashes, job loss scenarios
5. **Strategy Comparison**: Side-by-side comparison of different approaches
6. **Milestone Tracking**: "Am I on track for retirement at age 65?"
